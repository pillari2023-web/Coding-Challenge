**Sales Data Analysis – Excel Project
Project Overview**
This project simulates a real-world scenario where a Junior Data Analyst analyzes raw sales data for a retail company. The goal is to clean the dataset, calculate revenue, and generate insights about regional and product performance using Microsoft Excel.
The analysis helps the sales team understand which regions and products generate the most revenue, enabling better business decisions.
________________________________________
Business Scenario
In the IT and analytics industry, analysts often receive raw operational or sales data that must be processed and analyzed to generate meaningful insights.
In this project, the sales team shared raw sales data and requested an analysis of regional performance and product performance.
________________________________________
**Objectives**
The main objectives of this analysis are:
1.	Clean and prepare the raw dataset.
2.	Calculate Total Revenue using Excel formulas.
3.	Create Pivot Tables to analyze revenue.
4.	Identify Top 5 performing products.
5.	Create a visual chart to present regional revenue insights.
________________________________________
**Tools Used
**•	Microsoft Excel
•	Pivot Tables
•	Excel Formulas
•	Sorting and Filtering
•	Data Visualization (Charts)
________________________________________
Steps Performed
1. Data Cleaning
The dataset was checked for:
•	Missing values
•	Duplicate records
•	Formatting inconsistencies
Any issues found were corrected to ensure accurate analysis.
________________________________________
2. Revenue Calculation
A new column called Total Revenue was created.
Formula used:
Total Revenue = Quantity * Unit Price
This calculates the total sales value for each transaction.
Example Excel Formula:
=Quantity * Unit Price
________________________________________
3. Pivot Table Analysis
Total Revenue by Region
A Pivot Table was created with:
Rows:
•	Region
Values:
•	Sum of Total Revenue
This helps identify which regions contribute the most revenue.
________________________________________
Total Revenue by Product Category
Another Pivot Table was created with:
Rows:
•	Product Category
Values:
•	Sum of Total Revenue
This shows which product categories are the most profitable.
________________________________________
4. Top 5 Performing Products
Products were sorted by Total Revenue in descending order.
Using sorting and filtering, the Top 5 revenue-generating products were identified.
This helps the company focus on its most successful products.
________________________________________
5. Data Visualization
A Column Chart was created to visualize Revenue by Region.
The chart allows stakeholders to quickly compare regional performance and identify trends.
________________________________________
Key Insights
•	Certain regions generate significantly higher revenue.
•	Some product categories contribute more to total sales.
•	The Top 5 products represent the most valuable items for the business.
________________________________________
Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis:
•	Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
•	Promote top-performing products more aggressively.
•	Investigate and improve performance in low-revenue regions.
•	Maintain sufficient inventory for high-demand products.
